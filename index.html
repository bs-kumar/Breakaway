WE ARE BREAKAWAY

An agency built on a simple truth — to stand out, you need to stand apart. That’s when real impact happens.

We love ourselves a good challenge. And pride ourselves in not going down the conventional path to solving a business problem. But in fact, seek out a solution that's tailor-made for it.

And if need be, create something radically new.

We do this through our unique Dovetail Method, where we harness the power of collaboration and co-creation. Because we believe that how great things are born.

Yes, it’s scary. It’s daunting. But it’s also a brave step towards a brave new world. So finally, the question you need to ask yourself is— are you ready to Breakaway?

THE DOVETAIL METHOD

Dovetail: To 'fit together' or 'come together' in a harmonious or synergistic manner.

Unidimensional is dead. Long live diversity.

Today’s business problems require prying away from the comfortable, the typical.

So how do we achieve the atypical? In the most exciting way possible — by leveraging our network of disparate thinkers and creators from across diverse fields — collaborating, exploring, and co-creating with them — to address problems multi-dimensionally.

The outcome? Solutions that seamlessly meld together to disrupt the status quo and superglue the brand on the map.

Simply put, we help our clients break away.

We collaborate with world-class partners for: Films | Digital | Events | Activation | Everything else your brand needs
